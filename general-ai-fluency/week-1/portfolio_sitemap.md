### Claude's Response (Saved Output)

```
Pressure-test results for your 4-page portfolio:

You listed "3-4 backend case studies" but as a current intern,

If that section is thin or has placeholder content,
visitors will bounce before reaching Contact.

🔴 WHAT TO CHANGE:
Replace "3-4 case studies" with "2 deep case studies + 1 live project."
Depth beats breadth. One well-documented project with:
- The problem it solved
- The tech stack used
- A GitHub link or live demo
...is worth more than four bullet-point project names.

❌ WHAT TO REMOVE:
Nothing. Your 4 pages are justified. Do NOT add a "Skills" page —
your stack belongs on the About page, not its own page.

✅ ONE THING TO ADD:
A single social proof line on the Hero — even one line like:
"Currently interning at Flyrank, building AI-powered backend tools."
This answers the visitor's first question ("who is this person?")
before they even click to Work.
```
