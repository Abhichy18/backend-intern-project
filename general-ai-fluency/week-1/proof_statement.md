## How the AI Interview Sharpened This

The original sitemap claim was: *"I build AI-powered backend systems that ship fast and scale cleanly."*

That's two claims hiding in one ("ship fast" AND "scale cleanly"), and it targets no one specific. The pressure-test forced three cuts:

1. **"Ship fast" vs "scale cleanly" — which one?** → Chose "ship working things" because that's what an intern with 8 weeks of real deliverables can actually prove today. Clean architecture comes later.
2. **"AI-powered backend" is vague** → Narrowed to "REST APIs that plug AI tools in" — specific enough to be falsifiable, concrete enough to show up a portfolio page.
3. **"Contact me for internships/projects/collaborations" is three actions** → Cut to one: **email me.** The most direct, lowest-friction move a startup founder will make.
